Who we are?

  • About Us
  • Vision
  • Mission

We at Shreeja promote education for visually impaired students and all the Divyaangjan. And not just education, we also promote employement generation annd assist Divyaangjan to present themselves on a business platform, where they are manufacturers of some beautiful, creative and tasty products. Try it we are sure you will love it.

Shreeja believes in independency. We want to build such an environment where disability doesn't mean dependency. We want to provide resources that will create a sense of independency among Divyaangjan, starting from their childhood to all their life.

We aim to serve Divyaangjan in terms of accessibility in education, employment and business. We are creating a platform to learn, present and grow as a student, as a creator and as a business.
