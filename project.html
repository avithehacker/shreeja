Allow Us

Allow Us is a program initiated by Dharnika Foundation. Under this we support employment and livelihood of Divyaangjan. This is an online platform for sale of various consumer products by Divyaangjan, themselves. Here, you will get quality, taste, and better consumer experience. We aim not only to enhance quality of consumer products for the benefit of our consumers (customers), but also support the in-dependency of living among Divyaangjan. Be the reason of someone's livelihood, support Divyaang, support Humanity.

Veeery

Veeery is an eBook and audiobook online platform especially designed for the visually impaired children in order to help them to understand written text. India is home of more than 1/3rd of world’s visually impaired population. According to a report of NPCB India have more than 12 million individuals with visual impairment as against the global of 39 million. Out of which only 29.16% of blind are part of the education system – according to a survey by NCERT. In the same survey it was revealed that only 6.86% of schools have access to braille books and audio content. In a survey conducted by Veeery these are the list of problems we uncovered that visually impaired students face.
