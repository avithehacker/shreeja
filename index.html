About Us

We at Dharnika Foundation promote education for visually impaired students and all the Divyaangjan.

And not just education, we also promote employment generation and assist Divyaangjan to present themselves on a business platform, where they are manufacturers of some beautiful, creative and tasty products. Try it, we are sure you will love it.

Vision

Dharnika Foundation believes in independency. We want to build such an environment where disability doesn't mean dependency. We want to provide resources that will create a sense of independency among Divyaangjan, starting from their childhood to all their life.

Mission

We aim to serve Divyaangjan in terms of accessibility in education, employment and business. We are creating a platform to learn, present and grow as a student, as a creator and as a business.

How We Started

Our Story

The very first brick of Dharnika Foundation was laid by our First Founder, Avinash Singh. Although the Dharnika Foundation was founded in 2020, its journey had already begun in 2018 itself. Initially, Mr. Avinash had started building a (Website/App) platform for audiobook contents for students. In April 2019, Mr. Avinash met our Second Founder, Miss Mansi Jaiswal. When the whole thing got ready, they were ready to launch the website. But the idea drastically changed when Mr.Avinash met a Visually Divyaang in his native place. This interaction made Mr. Avinash realise how difficult life is for them, especially the education part. Lack of content and inaccessible format are big challenges in learning. A thought struck Avinash’s mind, that this very platform on which he was working on, could be a perfect solution for Visually Divyaangs. And this was it, now the whole idea shifted to Divyang. After that the whole concept was redesigned emphasising accessibility of the Platform. And this was the birth of one segment of Dharnika. As Mr. Avinash and Miss Mansi were working on accessibility of Idea, slowly esteem was created and many people joined, and a team took shape. The team slowly realised that not only Visually Divyangs face these problems but the all Divyangjans face issues relating to education and employment in their lives. Soon, in August 2020, the need for a well-structured organisation was felt and in September 2020, with founding members Mr. Avinash Singh and Miss Mansi Jaiswal, our very own Foundation was founded. Since then, Dharnika Foundation's working for the betterment of education and employment of the Divyangs.
