Dharnika Helping Hand

Kundan Kumar is a student of NIVH. He is blind and is from a poor family of Samastipur, Bihar. In this lockdown, when everybody was enjoying with family, there were many who had no money left and Kundan was one of them. Being entirely free he was looking for some source of income in order to support his family, therefore he created designer candles first time which he learned during his school times (in 2004) in a hope to earn something from its sale. But even after investing his all saving and scholarship, he was not able to sell majority of his products. Even after a lot of struggle and seeking for help, many people unfortunately took advantage of Kundan & they cheated him and ran away with half of his candles.

Let's Hear Kundan Story In His Own Words:- https://youtu.be/n72uW1V2SFQ
