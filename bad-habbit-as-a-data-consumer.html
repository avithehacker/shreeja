Bad habit as a data Consumer

Today I am writing a story about my friend Desouza, Desouza has a habit to watch youtube videos daily for few hours but one good thing is that He doesn't use to watch entertainment stuff he only watched informative content. Now in your mind, you have a question? What is the problem with that? After watching more and more content he is now addicted to those content. He is not productive in his work, whenever he is working he has to search on the web, then he gets distracted from his main objective and after a few hours later he starts thinking, what I am doing here why I am watching this content at the end of the day he finds out his whole time is wasted in watching content and his query remains same and objective does not fulfill.

So why does that happen to him? The answer is simple whenever some is watching something, youtube AI suggests him similar video now he is in the trap of AI he has to watch much similar content he enjoys this content but what is wrong with that he is not productive in real life. Information has no end you have to choose how much information is enough for you and what is your priority, the information or the work.

You have to choose what you want, do you want to succeed in work or you want to give more life span to this universe. More life span to this universe? The life span of the universe is directly proportional to the laziness of mankind. You want to know more, let's discuss it on another day.

Thanks for reading.

Author:- Avinash Singh
